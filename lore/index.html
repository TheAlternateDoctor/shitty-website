ABOUT THE WORLD

Our story takes places in a world very much like our own, in the 21st century.

In this universe, magic is a real thing; Sadly, it has been forgotten ages ago.
The Salem Witch Trials is the last time magic has been publicly seen.

Nowadays, only charlatans claims to do magic, while the few people with the ability to use it goes their whole lives without manifesting it.
